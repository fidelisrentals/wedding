Pre-Arrival to Colorado Instructions

Everyone coming from out of state NEEDS to drink at least 128oz of water within the first 24 hours of arriving. Then, 96oz of water each following day. It is recommended you get hydration packets to mix with your water to help with your body retaining the water. The ceremony at Pikes Peak will be at a altitude of 11,800 feet so it is likely you will get altitude sickness if you do not listen.

Local stores (walmart for example) sell oxygen bottles for about $10 that can help with altitude sickness if it were to occur. Recommended for our elders :)
